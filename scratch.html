Memories from My Mission – Oakland/San Francisco

My mission in the California Oakland/San Francisco Mission was incredible. I loved every second of it (well, most of the seconds anyways) and would go back in a heartbeat if I could. The memories I made and people I met will live with me forever. Even more powerful than these things, though, was the witness I gained of Jesus Christ and the Grace that He gives to us to find joy in this life and return to our Heavenly Home in the next.

Key Mission Experiences

  1. The Oakland Fruitvale Area
    • Seemed scary and dangerous at first, but full of life
    • People working hard for their families and futures
    • Many favorite people taught and served here
    • Now feels like home
  2. The Richmond South Area
    • Learned to spread my wings in the gospel
    • Felt Heavenly Father’s love deeply
    • Had sacred experiences with favorite companions
  3. The San Francisco Mission District
    • Struggled with city claustrophobia
    • Missionary work helped overcome discomfort
    • Made the decision to go "all in" on my mission
